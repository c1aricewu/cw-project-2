Biography.

I love to travel. Above is a picture of Niagara Falls from the first time I went. Though it is a frequented local spot, I am international student and it was new to me. Not only do I love to learn about cultures, I want to produce knowledge too.

I have been interested in research production since I was in grade 11, Higher Level Psychology for the International Baccalaureate diploma, where I reproduced a serial recall experiment and received a 6 (equivalent to an A) overall. My interest grew in the university courses that asked students to do individual research of academic journals when writing papers with loose areas of study. After receiving an good grade for my CCT208 research methods course, I knew this was the right path. I love learning about different subjects, and research projects are their own crash course of extensive multidisciplinary topics that will enrich and broaden my education.
